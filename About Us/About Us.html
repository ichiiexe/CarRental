About us

With over 70+ years in the business, we have earned the trust of our customers by the services we give. With up to 50 stations all over Greece and up to 1,000 locations worldwide, around the major cities and especially near airports and seaports. With a diverse selection of cars from SUVs, commercial cars and luxury cars. With the introduction of electric cars in the recent years, we have expanded our options with hybrid and electric cars. We promise you that our rental services will get you to your day trips, travels and meetings.

Our Mission

  • Customer Service: To provide excellent service online and offline.
  • Accesibility: To make it accesible and affordable to for individuals and businesses alike.
  • Flexibility: To adapt to the ever changing needs of our customers.
  • Sustainability: To provide eco-friendly options to help reduce our carbon footprint.

FAQ

How long is our leasing duration? Our leasing duration is around 1-4 years. How long you can lease our cars for will vary on your preferences and financial situation.

Can I end the duration of my lease? Yes, but it comes with a penalty cost. It's better to contact us so that we can understand what kind of situation is at hand.

What happens when I damage the car? If it is just a minor damage, no fees will be applied. On the other hand, if the damage is major, you may be responsible for the repair cost.

What happens at the end of the lease? There are some options you can have. Return,Extend or Purchase the car
