FAQs

1. What’s the difference between a car subscription and a traditional car lease?

keyboard_arrow_down

A car subscription is more flexible than a traditional lease. It includes everything—insurance, maintenance, and roadside assistance—in one monthly fee. Unlike a lease, you can switch cars, and there are no long-term commitments or large down payments.

2. How do I return the car if I no longer need it?

keyboard_arrow_down

Returning the car is simple. You can return it at any time according to your subscription terms, either at the end of your contract or earlier, with possible early termination fees. Our team will guide you through the return process.

3. Can I upgrade to a different car during my subscription?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes! You can upgrade or switch to a different model anytime during your subscription. Just contact us, and we’ll help you choose a new vehicle that suits your needs.

4. Are there any mileage restrictions?

keyboard_arrow_down

Each subscription comes with a set mileage allowance. If you exceed that limit, additional charges may apply. You can adjust your mileage plan before signing or at any time during your subscription.

5. What happens if the car breaks down?

keyboard_arrow_down

If your car breaks down or requires maintenance, simply contact us, and we’ll arrange for a replacement vehicle or get it fixed. Routine maintenance and roadside assistance are included in your subscription.
