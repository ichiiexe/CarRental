Royalty Points System

Our Royalty Points System is a way to reward our customers for their loyalty. Every time you make a purchase, you earn points that can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases. The more you spend, the more points you earn, and the more you save.

How to earn points

There are several ways to earn points. You can earn points by making a purchase, referring a friend, or sharing our website on social media. You can also earn points by participating in special promotions or events.

How to redeem points

To redeem your points, simply log in to your account and go to the Royalty Points section. From there, you can view your points balance and redeem your points for discounts on future purchases.
